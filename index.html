About Me

I'm Harsh Kumar, a versatile developer with a rich tapestry of experience weaving through diverse technological domains. My journey commenced in the dynamic realm of IoT and hardware programming, where I honed my skills in crafting robots and tinkering with circuits. As my curiosity and expertise expanded, I seamlessly transitioned into the world of software development, navigating the intricate landscapes of systems engineering, machine learning, cybersecurity, game development, full-stack, and backend development.

Over the course of my journey, I've etched my mark in the coding universe by actively participating in 40 hackathons, emerging victorious in an impressive 19 of them. Beyond the thrill of competition, I find immense joy in sharing my knowledge. Having mentored numerous individuals on their programming odysseys, I'm dedicated to fostering a collaborative and supportive coding community.

In the realm of game development, I've brought virtual worlds to life through extensive work in level design projects using Unreal Engine. On the backend frontier, I've left my fingerprints on various projects, leveraging Python Flask, Actix Rust, and Java Springboot to craft robust REST APIs. Additionally, my journey in machine learning has seen me contribute to diverse projects, showcasing the marriage of data and algorithms.
